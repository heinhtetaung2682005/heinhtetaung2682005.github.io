Major Achievements

  • 1999: Co-founded Alibaba Group to connect Chinese manufacturers with global buyers.
  • 2003: Launched Taobao, one of China’s largest online shopping platforms.
  • 2004: Started Alipay, a leading digital payment service.
  • 2014: Led Alibaba through the largest IPO in history at that time on the NYSE.
  • 2015: Alibaba became the world’s largest retail trading platform by gross merchandise volume.
  • 2019: Stepped down as Alibaba executive chairman to focus on philanthropy.

Recognition

Jack Ma is widely recognized for his vision of e-commerce, leadership in technology, and impact on global business.

Philanthropy

He created the Jack Ma Foundation to support education, environmental protection, and public health.
